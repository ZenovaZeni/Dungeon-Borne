# Asset Pipeline

## Art Style

Low-poly stylized dark fantasy with readable silhouettes and colorful magic.

Avoid realistic art.

## Asset Sources

Early prototype:
- primitives
- free/cheap low-poly packs
- Mixamo-style animations
- simple particle effects
- placeholder icons

Later:
- custom Blender edits
- unique weapons
- modular rooms
- boss models
- class-themed armor sets

## Model Rules

Keep models:
- low-poly
- readable on small screens
- strong silhouette
- minimal texture complexity
- optimized for mobile

## Weapon Visibility

Weapon models should be visible first.

Priority:
1. sword
2. axe
3. staff
4. bow
5. shield

## Character Gear Visibility

V1:
- base outfit by class
- weapon visible
- shield visible
- helmet optional
- armor color changes
- aura for class path

Later:
- more modular armor pieces

## Dungeon Pieces

Use modular pieces:
- floor tile
- wall
- corner
- doorway
- stairs
- gate
- chest
- torch
- altar
- boss door

## VFX Rules

VFX must be readable and mobile-safe:
- small particle counts
- simple shapes
- strong colors
- short duration
- clear hit impact

## Audio Rules

Even placeholder audio matters.

Add early:
- attack sound
- hit sound
- dash sound
- loot pickup sound
- level up sound
- enemy death sound
