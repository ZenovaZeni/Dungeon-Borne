# Companions and Arena

## Companions

Companions are a good idea, but not for the first combat prototype.

Add them after the player character combat feels good.

## Companion Concept

The player can recruit one companion at a time.

Companion roles:
- Defender
- Healer
- Archer
- Mage
- Rogue/Scout

Behavior modes:
- aggressive
- balanced
- defensive
- protect player
- focus boss
- stay back

Companion gear:
- weapon
- armor
- relic/accessory

Do not show all gear visually at first.
Use role-based outfit + weapon model + aura/color changes.

## Companion Revival

If companion dies:
- revive after combat
- revive with item
- revive at town
- or player can channel revive during battle

Use "Phoenix Down style" later if it fits the tone.

## Arena / PvP Roadmap

### Stage 1: Practice Arena
Player fights AI enemy waves.

### Stage 2: Build Arena
Player tests builds against class-based AI opponents.

### Stage 3: Ghost PvP
Players upload builds.
Other players fight AI-controlled versions of those builds.

Benefits:
- ranking system possible
- no real-time networking required
- cheaper and easier
- preserves PvP feeling

### Stage 4: Real-Time PvP
Only after combat, balance, player base, and backend are proven.

## PvP Design Rule

No pay-to-win.

Paid expansions can unlock content, but ranked PvP needs fairness:
- normalize stats
- separate PvE power from PvP power if needed
- cosmetic monetization is safer
